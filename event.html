Old Money Run

The Old Money Run returns to Tattenhoe Sports Pavilion for its second year. Exclusive to paid members of Redway Runners, the Old Money Run is a fantastic event, perfect for runners moving from 5k to 10k or 10k to a half, or for those preparing for longer distances.

What

10 miles and 5 miles options
Exclusive to Redway Runners members

When

Sunday 22 September 2019
Start time: 10.00am

Where

Tattenhoe Sports Pavilion, The Pavilion, Holborn Cres, Tattenhoe, Milton Keynes MK4 3EQ

Entry

10 miles: £12.50, 5 miles: £9.50
plus booking fee.

Register now
Minimum age is 14 years old on the day of the race. Entries close 23.59 on 15 September 2019, or earlier if sold out.

Route

We have 5 or 10 mile options, both starting at Tattenhoe Sports Pavilion taking in the scenic views of West Milton Keynes. The route follows the brook from Tattenhoe through the beautiful Parks Trust land, passing through Emerson Valley to Furzton and round the lake and back.

Award

To commemorate your achievement all finishers will receive a unique and fabulous medal.

Useful info

Event packs will not be sent out prior to the day so please allow plenty of time to collect packs on the day. The run will begin at 10.00. Entries are limited to 125 per distance.

There will be parking and toilet facilities at Tattenhoe Sport Pavilion. Additional parking will be outside Gilesbrook School. The café at Tattenhoe Pavilion will be open for refreshments.

Entries close at 23.59 on Sunday 15 September 2019, or earlier if sold out. Entry fees are 12 Pounds 10 at Shillings (£12.50) per runner for 10 miles and 9 Pounds 10 Shillings (£9.50) per runner for 5 miles, plus booking fee. Minimum age is 14 years old on the day of the race.

Volunteers and runners need to sign up by logging into Loveadmin. Queries to Daria at oldmoneyrun@redwayrunners.com
