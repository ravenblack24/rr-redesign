Who we are

image goes here
Redway Runners is an award-winning community running club based in Milton Keynes. From humble beginnings back in 2011, the club now has over 3.600 paid members making it one of the largest running clubs in the UK. Above all, Redway Runners is built on having fun and getting fit in a friendly environment that welcomes all abilities.
